Placement Process

{ Image courtesy : Google }

  1. Students are required to register on the platform in their 1st year. As a student, you are required to visit the portal regularly for any relevant updates.

  2. Thereafter, they are required to update their semester results, as when they receive them, generally, annually. Students should also keep updating and uploading their resume.

  3. In their third last semester, generally 6th semester, students are mandated to get their qualifications and other documents verified by their college's Training & Placement Cell faculty.

  4. Interested and eligible students apply to be considered for the job.

  5. Companies get access to the resume and profile of the interested students through the T&P website and students are shortlisted.

  6. Shortlisted students are notified.

  7. Students can check their progress in various rounds of the placement session.

  8. After completion of selection process, the selected students are notified.

Why use our platform?

  1. Enter your qualifications and upload your resume for attracting recruiters.

  2. Pre-define your minimum expected salary and get notification of only those recruiters offering more than that.

  3. Get notified through email and message about various placement related information by the college.

  4. Contact college's training and placement cell officers directly for various placement related queries.

  5. Get notifications about the result of the placement rounds by the recruiters.
