Placement Process

{ Image courtesy : Google }

  1. Placement office sends invitations to companies/organizations along with relevant information.

  2. Companies and organizations interested to recruit, register to the T&P website.

  3. A placement session can be created by both the college and the company by sending association requests to each other. The job profile and elegibility criteria is decided at this step after the request is accepted.

  4. Interested and eligible students apply to be considered for the job. Recruiters can see all the applications on the platform.

  5. Recruiters get access to the resume and profile of the interested students through the T&P website and students are shortlisted.

  6. Recruiters can notify the students and the information is sent to the college regarding the notification. Shortlisted students are notified.

  7. Recruiters can inform the college or the students regarding results of various rounds.

  8. After completion of selection process, recruiters can notify the selected students.

Why use our platform?

  1. Send a direct request to the training and placement cell of the college for organizing a recruitment drive after signing up on the platform.

  2. Pre-define the job criteria for the drive and only the elligible students can apply.

  3. Get access to the resume and profile of the interested students.

  4. Notify students and college about the result of various rounds of placement process.

  5. After the drive is complete, notify the selected students about the result.
