Placement Process

  1. Placement office sends invitations to companies/organizations along with relevant information.

  2. Companies and organizations interested to recruit, register to the T&P website.

  3. A placement session can be created by both the college and the company by sending association requests to each other. The job profile and elegibility criteria is decided at this step after the request is accepted.

  4. Interested and eligible students apply to be considered for the job.

  5. Companies get access to the resume and profile of the interested students through the T&P website and students are shortlisted.

  6. Shortlisted students are notified.

  7. Students can check their progress in various rounds of the placement session.

  8. After completion of selection process, the selected students are notified.
